// FAQ

Common Questions

What cryptocurrencies are accepted? +
Pi Network (recommended), USDT, Solana, TRON, BSC, TON, Ethereum, and Bitcoin. Pi Network gives a 2% fee discount.
How does the escrow system work? +
Client's funds are locked in a smart contract escrow. The freelancer works knowing payment is secured. Upon delivery approval, funds release automatically. If dispute arises, our DAO resolves it.
What are the platform fees? +
Web3 Weapon charges 10% on completed orders. Pi Network payments receive a 2% discount — making it just 8% for Pi users. Lowest fees in Web3 freelancing.
Can I use the platform on mobile? +
Yes! Fully responsive and mobile-first. A PWA is available for installation on your phone for native-like experience.
How do I get verified as a freelancer? +
Connect your crypto wallet, complete email verification, and optionally submit ID for verified badge. Verified freelancers get 3× more visibility in search results.
